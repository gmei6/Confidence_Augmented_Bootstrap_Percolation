# Advisor Prep

Produce a meeting packet for the ~monthly advisor check-in. Honor GEMINI.md and AGENTS.md.

## Steps
1. Read `docs/PROJECT_TRACKER.md` §2, §8 (latest validated results), §9 (open questions —
   especially Q2/Q3 flagged for the advisor), §10, and the relevant `docs/research/` files.
2. Have `research-analysis` draft a concise briefing in `docs/research/` (e.g.
   `advisor_brief_<date>.md`) containing:
   - One-paragraph status against the North Star and roadmap (§6).
   - The latest phase diagram / threshold comparison and what it shows (reference the figure
     in `results/figures/`; do not recompute).
   - The open questions to raise (Q2: G(n,p) vs configuration model; Q3: critical-window
     framing) with a crisp recommendation or the decision needed from the advisor.
   - Any blocker needing input (e.g. PACE access).
3. Keep it honest and lean (§0 rule 3) — no optimistic fiction; flag uncertainty.
4. Present the draft for approval; do not edit the tracker (`research-analysis` is advisory).
